A Sanitary Environment


Most people don't understand just how prone to bacteria the average hot tub really is. That's because they don't see the large, complex maze of pipes that are used in traditional hot tubs and bubble tubs. There are literally dozens of feet of pipes, tubes and nozzles on most of these tubs. And all of that excessive piping can end up becoming a hot bed of bacterial growth. Simply cleaning these tubs is not enough, as the bacteria can easily grow in the internal piping.

Thankfully, Sanijet tubs are designed completely different than traditional hot tubs. The unique design of these hot tubs does not include excessive pipes. That means that these tubs are inherently cleaner and more sterile than traditional bubble tubs or hot tubs. You want your birthing environment to be as sanitary and free from bacteria as possible, and that is precisely what you get when you choose a birthing facility that uses Sanijet Tubs.

More Relaxing
